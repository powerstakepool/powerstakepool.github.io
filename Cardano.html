What is Cardano?

Cardano is an open-source Proof Of Stake (POS) 3rd Generation blockchain network. ADA is the native digital currency on Cardano network and was named after Ada Lovelace who is believed to be the first computer programmer. Anyone who holds ADA in a wallet can stake Ada and earn rewards.
